Support

We strive to make our documentation as clear as possible, but sometimes things can get confusing. Good news â€“ our documentation is open-source! You can view and contribute to it on github.

How to help improve our documentation:

  • For typos, grammar, or other errors, we'd appreciate your support! Simply fork our repo, make the necessary changes, and submit a pull request.
  • If you have questions or need help, feel free to create an issue or join the community in the robotics channel on prefix.dev's discord.

We're always eager to improve, and your input is valuable to us. Thank you for being part of the RoboStack community!

Experiencing ROS Distro-Specific Issues?

If you're facing any issues specific to a ROS distribution, we recommend creating an issue in the corresponding GitHub repository. You can also contribute by following the guidelines in our contribution guide.

  • ROS Noetic
  • ROS2 Humble
  • ROS2 Jazzy
  • ROS2 Kilted
  • ROS2 Galactic (not actively maintained anymore)
  • ROS2 Foxy (not actively maintained anymore)

By submitting your issue to the appropriate repo, we can better understand and address your concerns. Thank you for helping us improve the RoboStack ecosystem!
